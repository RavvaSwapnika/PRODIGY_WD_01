50 Years of serving communities

Starbucks had been serving communities for around 50 years since its inception in 1971. Over these decades, it has grown from a single store in Seattle's Pike Place Market to a global phenomenon with thousands of locations worldwide.

From pioneering the concept of a "third place" between home and work to its commitment to ethical sourcing and sustainability, Starbucks has left an indelible mark on the coffee industry and the communities it serves.
